## GAME START

Every Monday is a brand new game.
Every day is a new level.
Every good choice earns you XP.
Stack enough XP and you unlock awesome stuff.

**Ready? Let's go.**

---

## DAILY QUESTS

These are missions you can complete every single day. Each one earns you XP. A perfect day is worth 15 or more XP!

### Morning Quests

Complete these before you leave for school.

| Quest | XP |
|---|:---:|
| Wake up without drama | +1 XP |
| Make your bed | +1 XP |
| Get dressed on your own | +1 XP |
| Finish breakfast on time | +1 XP |
| Ready for school on time | +1 XP |

### School Quests

Your teacher tracks these with the behavior clip. You report your color when you get home.

| Quest | XP |
|---|:---:|
| Behavior clip stays on green | +3 XP |
| Behavior clip moves ABOVE green | +5 XP |
| Homework done without a fight | +2 XP |

*Note: If your clip goes above green, you get +5 XP (not +3 and +5). It replaces it.*

### Evening Quests

Complete these between after-school and bedtime.

| Quest | XP |
|---|:---:|
| Eat dinner on time (no dragging it out) | +1 XP |
| Follow instructions the first time | +1 XP |
| Be kind and have a positive attitude | +1 XP |
| Bedtime routine done on time | +1 XP |
| Read for at least 10 minutes | +1 XP |

---

## BONUS QUESTS

These are special missions. They can happen anytime and they're worth extra XP. Think of them as power-ups.

| Quest | XP |
|---|:---:|
| Help someone without being asked | +2 XP |
| Handle frustration calmly (deep breaths, walk away, use words) | +2 XP |
| Accept "no" without a tantrum or argument | +2 XP |
| Do something extra kind or put in extra effort | +3 XP |

:::tip[PRO TIP]
Bonus Quests are the secret to having a Gold Week. Look for chances to earn them every day!
:::

---

## TAKING DAMAGE

Just like in a game, some choices cost you. These take XP away from your daily score. The good news: you can always earn more XP tomorrow.

| What Happened | Damage |
|---|:---:|
| Behavior clip drops to yellow | −2 XP |
| Behavior clip drops to orange | −4 XP |
| Behavior clip hits red | −6 XP |
| Not listening after being asked twice | −1 XP |
| Screaming or having a tantrum | −2 XP |
| Being disrespectful | −2 XP |
| Lying | −3 XP |

:::note[IMPORTANT]
The most damage you can take in one day is −8 XP. And XP you earned earlier in the day is always safe — it can't be taken away.
:::

## THE REWARD SHOP

This is the fun part. You save up XP in your Reward Bank and spend it on rewards. Your Reward Bank carries over from week to week, so you can save up for the really big stuff!

**How it works:** Every day, half of the XP you earned goes into your Reward Bank.

Check out the full [Reward Shop](/rewards/shop/) to see everything you can buy!

---

## YOUR WEEKLY LEVEL

At the end of every week (Sunday night), your total XP decides what level you hit. Each level unlocks different stuff for the next week.

### GOLD WEEK — 80+ XP

Everything is unlocked! Full screen time, all activities, PLUS you get a bonus reward from the shop. This is the level you're going for.

### GREEN WEEK — 60 to 79 XP

Great week! Full screen time, all activities, everything's good. This is a solid level.

### YELLOW WEEK — 40 to 59 XP

You still keep all your activities (swimming, rock climbing), but screen time gets cut in half on the weekend. You can do better next week!

### RED WEEK — Below 40 XP

No fun screens for the weekend. You still get swimming and rock climbing though — those never go away. Reset on Monday and come back stronger!

---

## GAME RULES

1. Every Monday the weekly XP resets to zero. Brand new game, brand new chance.
2. Your Reward Bank does NOT reset. You keep saving those up.
3. XP you earn in the morning is locked in. Even if you have a tough afternoon, the morning XP stays.
4. Swimming and rock climbing can never be taken away. Ever.
5. You can spend from your Reward Bank anytime — just ask Mom or Dad.
6. YOU get to write your own XP on the board when you earn it.
