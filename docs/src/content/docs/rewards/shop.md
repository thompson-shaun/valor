title: Reward Shop
description: Browse all the rewards you can buy with your XP.
---

Save up XP in your Reward Bank and spend it here! Every day, half your earned XP goes into the bank automatically.

---

## Weekday Rewards (Mon–Fri)

| Reward | Cost |
|---|:---:|
| Extra 30 min screen time | 10 XP |
| Small treat (candy, snack) | 10 XP |
| Stay up 20 min past bedtime | 15 XP |

## Weekend Rewards (Sat–Sun)

| Reward | Cost |
|---|:---:|
| Boba or special drink | 20 XP |
| Pick the family movie | 20 XP |
| Extra 1 hour screen time | 25 XP |
| Pick dinner for the family | 30 XP |
| Stay up 30 min past bedtime | 15 XP |
| Disney trip (3–4 hours) | 40 XP |

## Save-Up Rewards (anytime!)

| Reward | Cost |
|---|:---:|
| Small toy or book (under $15) | 50 XP |
| Special outing (just him + parent) | 60 XP |
| Big reward (new game, experience) | 100 XP |
| **Nintendo Switch 2** | **250 XP** |

---

:::tip[The Switch 2 Goal]
250 XP is the ultimate boss-level reward. With strong daily play, it takes roughly 5–7 weeks of saving. You can do it!
:::
